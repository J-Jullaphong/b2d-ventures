Problem

Global markets often face a shortage of high-quality salmon due to seasonal fluctuations, regional availability, and sustainability concerns. This affects not only direct consumers but also restaurants and retailers who rely on consistent supply for their operations.

Product

Salmon Lake aims to provide a consistent, sustainable supply of high-quality salmon to international markets. Utilizing eco-friendly aquaculture practices, the company will offer fresh, frozen, and smoked salmon, ensuring high nutritional value and exceptional taste, meeting the diverse needs of global customers.

Location

The company will be based near coastal regions with access to pristine water conditions ideal for salmon farming. This strategic location facilitates easy export logistics and ensures the freshness of the salmon delivered to various markets. The proximity to major shipping routes will also reduce transportation costs and environmental impact.

Founder

The founder, Alex Morgan, is an experienced aquaculture specialist with over a decade of experience in marine biology and sustainable fishing practices. Alex's vision for Salmon Lake is to merge traditional aquaculture with innovative technologies to set new standards for sustainability and quality in the salmon industry.
