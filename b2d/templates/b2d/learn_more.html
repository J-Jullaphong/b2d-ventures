FAQs

For Investors:

  • How do I sign up as an investor? You can sign up by clicking the "Login" button on the homepage and click the "Sign Up" on the login page and selecting the "Investor" option. Fill in your details and submit your request. Once approved by the admin, you’ll be able to log in and start browsing business.
  • How do I find businesses to invest in? After logging in, you can use the Business Browsing System to search for businesses based on various filters.
  • What type of information can I expect to see in a business profile? Business profiles include detailed information such as market analysis, financial health, growth potential, team, and other key metrics that will help you evaluate investment opportunities.
  • How do I invest in a business? After viewing the business details, you can click on the “Invest in Business” button. Follow the steps, including specifying your investment amount and completing the transaction via QR code.
  • Is my investment guaranteed? No, investments carry inherent risks, and there is no guarantee of returns. Be sure to thoroughly evaluate each business before investing.
  • Can I track the progress of my investments? Yes, you can access your portfolio through your dashboard, where you can track all your current investments, including the amount invested and the progress of each business.
  • How do I withdraw my investment? Depending on the business agreement and the stage of the business, withdrawals or returns on your investment may vary. Contact the business owner or refer to the investment terms for details.
  • Who approves my investment? All investments are reviewed and approved by the admin to ensure compliance with platform regulations.
  • What happens if a business fails? If a business fails, you may lose your investment. It is important to review the business thoroughly and understand the risks involved.
  • How is my data secured? B2D Ventures uses secure encryption and other security measures to protect your personal and financial data.

For Business Owners:

  • How do I sign up as a business owner? You can sign up by selecting the "Business" tab during the registration process. Provide your business details and upload the required documents (such as tax ID, business registration certificates). Once approved by the admin, you can access your account.
  • How do I create a business profile? After logging in, go to the “Business Profile” section, where you can enter details about your business, including description, category, and financial data. You can also upload images, videos, and other promotional material.
  • How do I start a fundraising campaign? Once your business profile is complete, you can create a fundraising campaign by specifying your funding goal, number of shares, Minimum Shares, Share Type, publish date, and deadline. Your campaign will be subject to admin approval.
  • Can I update my business profile after launching a campaign? Yes, you can make updates to your business profile at any time, though certain changes to your campaign may require admin approval.
  • How do I track the progress of my fundraising? You can view the progress of your campaign on your dashboard, including details like total funds raised, number of investors, and the status of individual investments.
  • How do I communicate with potential investors? The platform will provide the contract for you, allowing you to share additional information and updates about your business.
  • Can I raise funds more than once? Yes, you can launch new fundraising campaigns after the current campaign has ended.
  • How secure is the platform for my business information? B2D Ventures takes data security seriously, using encryption and industry-standard security measures to protect your business information.

Investment Risks

Note: Investing in startups and early-stage businesses carries significant risks. You should carefully evaluate the following risks before investing.

  • Loss of Capital The business may fail, leading to a complete or partial loss of your investment.
  • Illiquidity Investments in startups are generally illiquid, meaning you might not be able to withdraw or sell your shares quickly or at all.
  • Business Risk Each business faces unique risks related to its operations, market competition, and external factors.
  • No Guarantee of Returns There is no guarantee of receiving dividends or that the business will succeed in generating returns.
  • Long-Term Horizon Investments in startups are typically long-term and may take years to mature before you see any returns, if any.
  • Regulatory and Legal Risks Changes in regulations or laws could negatively affect the business or its ability to succeed.
